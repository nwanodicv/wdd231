The Ogwashi-uku Chamber of Commerce

We are dedicated to supporting and promoting local businesses in Ogwashi-uku and its surrounding areas. Our chamber serves as a hub for networking, resources, and advocacy to help our members thrive in today's competitive market.

As a member of our chamber, you will have access to a variety of benefits, including:

  • Networking events and opportunities to connect with other local business owners
  • Access to business resources and educational workshops
  • Advocacy on behalf of local businesses at the municipal and regional levels
  • Marketing and promotional opportunities through our chamber website and events
